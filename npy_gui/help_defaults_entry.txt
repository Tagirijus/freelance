Default entries are strings, float and booleans like this:


Strings:
	Title, comment and amount format.

Amount format has replacement values like this:

- {d}: amount in decimal
- {H}: amount converted to minutes, show hours
- {M}: amount converted to minutes, show minutes
- {S}: amount converted to minutes, show seconds

Example for a amount of 91.75:
"{d} pieces" = "91.75 pieces"
"{H}:{M} hours" = "1:31 hours"
"{M}:{S} minutes" = "91:45 minutes"
"{H}:{M}:{S} hours" = "1:31:45 hours"


Float:
	Amount, Time, h-rate (hourly rate), multi (multiplicator)


Boolean:
	is_time
